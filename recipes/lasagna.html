Lasagna

Description

A delicious lasagna that will sure make your guests' mouths water when it comes out the oven! It's so creamy and delicious that people will think that you used 30 pounds of butter while making it. No need with this recipe!

Please do not use 30 pounds of butter on this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • White Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
