Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a pizza you can make easily at home. Please do not make this pizza in the presence of any italians.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Marinara Sauce (from an Italian friend)
  • Pepperoni
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Sliced Black Olives

Steps

  1. Place pizza dough on a baking surface
  2. Preheat oven to 400F
  3. Pour a thin layer of marinara over all but the edges of the dough
  4. Spread shredded mozzarella in the same manner
  5. Place pepperoni and olives to taste, or other toppings
  6. Bake until cheese is melty and delicious
  7. Rest for at least 10 minutes before serving
  8. Serve in anonymity, away from Italians
